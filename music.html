Music

Here's a bunch of the music I've made since I started producing in high school! Feel free to give it a listen or just scroll through my progression as a producer!

ls. · middle mile
This is the first bunch of tracks I ever released on my SoundCloud! I tried to make lo-fi hip-hop, using a bunch of different samples I found digging through YouTube including jazz, OST, and assorted music videos.
ls. · plaintive
Here's another track I made in high school, using a guitar sample I found online.
Fun Fact: this is the track I used as my backing soundtrack for the video portfolio I applied to Brown with!
ls. · depressionfruit
Here's a flip of Drake's Passionfruit I made by replacing the original instrumental to the song with another guitar sample I found, as well as recreating the drums. My intention was to make a sadder version of the song to listen to.
ls. · scene change
Here's a beat tape I made over the course of right before and during COVID, with a bunch of different techniques I wanted to try using, such as emulating producers I was listening to during this period, or new production techniques.
ls. · who said
Here's another track I made as part of an event during COVID! I tried copying this track from Joji (one of my favorite artists) in terms of chopping up a sample to make a catchy beat.
ls. · incend
I made this track while I was taking MUSC 0200, by sampling different pieces of music made from local Providence artists and trying to make a catchy beat.
ls. · weepy
Here's my final project for MUSC 0200; I tried doing another flip of different songs I liked (including Drake's Passionfruit) but with greater production knowledge and including more vocal tracks.
ls. · mantras tape
Here's the most recent batch of music I made- I produced most of these songs this last summer of 2022, as I wouldn't have access to production software during the school semester, so I tried to make something I enjoyed before I came back to Brown. I tried producing more lo-fi hip-hop but also going outside of my comfort zone and working in different genres.
